# Technical Report: Reactor Thermal-Hydraulic Analysis

## Introduction
Thermal-hydraulics plays a critical role in nuclear reactor safety by ensuring efficient heat removal from the reactor core.

## Equation Used
Q = m × c × ΔT

## Assumptions
- Steady-state operation
- Constant specific heat
- No heat losses

## Discussion
The analysis shows a linear relationship between coolant flow rate and heat removal. This highlights the importance of maintaining adequate coolant circulation in reactor systems.

## Conclusion
Efficient cooling is essential to prevent overheating and ensure safe reactor operation. Flow rate optimization is a key design parameter.
